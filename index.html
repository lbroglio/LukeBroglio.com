About Me:

Hello, I'm Luke Broglio. I'm a student at Iowa State University studying Software Engineering with a minor in Cybersecurity. I grew up in the Twin Cities, Minnesota, where I fell in love with programming at a young age. I spent many of my middle and high school summers learning to make games in Python, Unity Engine, and Unreal.

I'm experienced in C, C++, C#, Python, Java, HTML/CSS, and Javascript. My personal favorites languages are C++ and Python. I'm also proficient in Git and have worked in Agile development.

I'm passionate about the power of technology to solve complex problems and create new opportunities. In my future career, I plan to develop innovative software applications that have a real impact on the world around me.

When not studying or working on programming projects, I like to keep up with new technological developments, get involved on campus, spend time with friends, and read Science Fiction novels.
